Who We Are

Founded in 2013, Refcool Services Limited (RSL) is an electromechanical contracting company that focuses on providing high-quality installations and services, tailored to our client's needs and budget. We specialize in HVAC, electrical, mechanical installations, and maintenance. With a qualified team of engineers and technicians, we are committed to delivering excellence in every project we undertake.

Our Mission

We aim to achieve our vision through continual innovation, using modern technology to ensure quality and excellence in all our services. We prioritize safety and strive to attract and retain the best talent in the industry.

Our Vision

Our goal is to achieve regional domination and establish a national presence in electromechanical consulting, project execution, installation, and supply, while maintaining a flexible structure offering differentiated services.
